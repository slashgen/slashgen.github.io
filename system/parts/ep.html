emotional parts

emotional parts are the parts of the personality that represent the dissociation or that contain the traumatic materials (memories of the trauma , internalized beliefs and perceptions , learned responses , etc ) . emotional parts are often drawn forth by reminders of the trauma and may not experience much of everyday life . because of this , they might be far less developed than ANP .
